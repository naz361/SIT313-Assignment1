Corned beef with mustard parsley sauce

INGREDIENTS

1.5kg corned beef 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar 2 tablespoons brown sugar 1 medium brown onion, roughly chopped 4 cloves 1/2 teaspoon whole black peppercorns 2 dried bay leaves 500g chat potatoes 1 bunch Dutch (baby) carrots, trimmed, peeled steamed green beans, to serve 50g butter 2 tablespoons plain flour 2 cups milk 2 teaspoons wholegrain mustard 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves

METHODS

Step 1

Place beef in a large saucepan. Cover with cold water. Add vinegar, sugar, onion, cloves, peppercorns and bay leaves. Cover. Bring to the boil over medium-high heat.

Step 2

Reduce heat to low. Simmer, partially covered, for 1 hour 30 minutes. Add potato. Simmer, partially covered, for 30 minutes or until potato is tender, adding carrots in the last 5 minutes. Remove from heat.

Step 3

Meanwhile, make mustard parsley sauce: Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour. Cook, stirring for 1 minute or until bubbling. Remove from heat. Gradually stir in milk until combined. Return to heat. Cook, stirring for 3 to 4 minutes or until mixture boils and thickens. Remove from heat. Stir in mustard and parsley.

Step 4

Remove beef from pan. Slice. Serve with sauce, potato, carrots, and beans.
